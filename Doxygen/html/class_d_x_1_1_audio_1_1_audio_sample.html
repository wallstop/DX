Detailed Description

AudioSamples are byte packages of an individual sample of some audio waveform. The actual bitwise-makeup of any given sample is defined by an AudioFormat. AudioSamples should NEVER be orphaned from an AudioPacket - they hold simple references to memory held by an AudioPacket, and have undefined (bad!) behavior if kept around past the lifetime of their parent AudioPacket.

AudioSamples, for most cases, should never be directly accessed. Their primary purpose is to provide a nice abstraction for Filters (DSPs) converting waveforms
